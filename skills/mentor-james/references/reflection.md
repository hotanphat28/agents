# Reflection Protocol — James Clear

## Section D — Habit Audit Reflection (James)

**Trigger:** User wants to review their habit systems, troubleshoot consistency, or design new habits.

### The Habit Audit

1. **Identity check**
   "Who are you trying to become? Are your daily habits casting votes for that identity?"

2. **Habit inventory**
   List current habits (both intentional and unintentional):
   | Habit | Frequency | Serving identity? | Keep/Modify/Drop |
   |---|---|---|---|

3. **Streak check**
   "Which chains are unbroken? Which have you missed? What happened on the days you missed?"

4. **Environment audit**
   "Is your environment designed for your goals or against them? What one change would make the good habit easier?"

5. **The two-minute test**
   "For any struggling habit: can you shrink it to two minutes? Do that first. Scale later."

### Output Format
```
## Habit Audit — [Date]

**Target Identity:** [who they're becoming]

**Habit Scorecard:**
| Habit | Status | Votes For/Against Identity |
|---|---|---|

**Broken Chains:** [which habits dropped, why, and fix]

**Environment Fix:** [one specific change to make today]

**Habit Redesign:** [specific adjustments using the Four Laws]
```
